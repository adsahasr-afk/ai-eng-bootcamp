# Acme Robotics Employee Handbook (Sample, Synthetic)

Acme Robotics was founded in 2019 and is headquartered in Portland.

Employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per year, plus public
holidays. The standard work week is 37.5 hours. Core collaboration hours are
10:00 to 15:00 local time.

Remote work is permitted up to three days per week with manager approval.

The company reimburses up to 1000 dollars per year for professional development,
including online courses and conference tickets.

All production database access requires two-factor authentication and every
access is logged for audit purposes.
